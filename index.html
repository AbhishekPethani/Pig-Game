Rules

1. The game has two players and two dice, playing in rounds.
2. In each turn, a player rolls both dice as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to his ROUND score.
3. BUT, if the player get a 1 in either dice, all his ROUND score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn.
4. The player can choose to 'Hold', which means his ROUND score gets added to his GLOBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn.
5. The player who reach target points on GLOBAL score wins the game.
